Our Founder

I’m Sean Prenter, a disabled educator and the founder of Access All Areas, with nearly a decade of experience supporting organisations and students across Aotearoa. My work combines lived experience, policy expertise, and practical tools that help young people navigate their school, social, and future with confidence. I created AAA to make support clearer, kinder, and easier to use for everyone involved.

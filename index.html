Who we are

Access All Areas (AAA) is a youth-led initiative empowering disabled young people across Aotearoa.

Vision

We envision an Aotearoa where disabled youth are confident leaders.

Why this matters

Disabled students face systemic barriers in education and employment.

Our offer

  • Equip disabled students with confidence
  • Support identity development
  • Foster leadership and advocacy

Key themes

  • Shared experience
  • Friendships
  • Accessing support

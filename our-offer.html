Our Offer: Accessing Futures

  • Empowering: students to speak about their lived experience with clarity and access support with self-assurance
  • Learning: from disabled students, enabled by the trust and shared experience of a disabled youth facilitator
  • Fostering: a future where disabled students envision an empowered future
  • Supporting: identity development through storytelling, reflection, and peer connection
  • Belonging: to a cohort of peers with a shared experience
  • Inspiring: students with visible role models which they can see their experience within
  • Equipping: students with tools and strategies to confidently navigate social, academic, and vocational spaces

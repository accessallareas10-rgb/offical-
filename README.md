    <h2>Why This Matters: A Snapshot of Urgent Need</h2>
    <img src="Picture 3.jpg" alt="Student excluded from group circle" class="image" />
    <ul>
      <li>Nearly half of disabled students report unmet needs in education</li>
      <li>6 in 10 disabled students are bullied</li>
      <li>Twice as likely to leave school with no qualifications</li>
      <li>Four times more likely to not be in education, employment, or training</li>
      <li>72% of unemployed disabled adults want to work</li>
    </ul>
    <p>These outcomes reflect environments that often fail to affirm disabled identities.</p>
